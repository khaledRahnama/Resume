About Me

I am a software engineer with a multidisciplinary background and over 15 years of experience across electronics, software development, and AI. My academic journey includes an ASc in Electronics, a BSc in Software Engineering and an MSc in Artificial Intelligence, which have supported my ability to work on diverse projects in both hardware and software domains.

I started my career as an Electronics Technician, diagnosing and repairing various systems like household appliances and CNC machines. Later, I worked as a Field Engineer, maintaining and troubleshooting non-destructive testing (NDT) equipment. My experience also includes designing electronic devices, developing custom AVR boards, and integrating hardware with software as part of my role as a Software Engineer.

In addition to electronics, I’ve contributed to software projects, including developing applications with various technologies, creating intuitive user interfaces, and implementing RESTful APIs. I also have experience in machine learning, deep learning, computer vision, and statistical analysis for market behavior studies.

I enjoy solving complex problems and learning new technologies. With my broad experience and adaptability, I aim to bring value to teams tackling challenges in technology, innovation, and development.
